Why Start with Kana?

Hiragana and Katakana (collectively called Kana) are the foundation of Japanese writing. Mastering these phonetic alphabets is your first step toward Japanese fluency. Unlike Kanji, each Kana character represents a specific sound, making them the perfect starting point.
